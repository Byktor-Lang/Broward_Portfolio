Paso 4 — Negocia la descripción (the complication)
Desafío / Challenge
You described el suegro (Papá Roberto) as estricto pero justo. Your partner has no physical description of him. Together you must choose exactly two adjectives you both agree describe him completely and accurately. You must both be satisfied — discuss and negotiate in Spanish!
Creo que el suegro es ______ porque ______. · ¿Estás de acuerdo con ______?

Our agreed description (both partners write this): El suegro de Jorge es ______ y ______.

Paso 5 — Síntesis: el retrato de la familia
